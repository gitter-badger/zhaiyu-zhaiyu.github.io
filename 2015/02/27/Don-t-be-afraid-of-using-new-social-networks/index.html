Don't be afraid of using new social networks

Feb 27 2015

That was a month ago, I find Telegram is a good new tool to communicate with my friends. The encryption and privacy things are all satisfying me.

I signed up…I had an account…I had no contact in it

I know, it’s always a problem that none of your friend join in. For me, it’s a problem.

At least, the same problem came up when I am ready to use Path.

We are not leader in a little group, even we are, we would not use the tiny power to force our friends to use a new tool. What we do is listing the advantages of the new tool and persuade friends into using it. Fail a lot? Aye, that’s true.

But here are some reasons for you to use new communicating tool or network:

  • The reason why you are willing to use the new thing.
  • The same habit and attitude towards innovation shared between you and your friend.
  • The fact that a true loving friend would try your suggestion and even there is only one contact in his/her list, which is you, he/she would keep the app in the phone.

I would call the 3 lines above Y.’s Standard of Friendship.

So I have few friends.
